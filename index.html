Artificial Intelligence

"Artificial Intelligence: the innovation that will change the world"

Artificial intelligence (AI) is an interdisciplinary field of science concerned with the development of algorithms and systems that can simulate human intelligence, enabling machines to learn, reason, perceive and act autonomously. AI is based on algorithmic learning machines, neural networks, optimization and pattern recognition algorithms. AI has applications in many fields, including robotics, cybersecurity, medicine, finance, manufacturing industry, office automation, games, and many others. AI has the potential to improve the lives of people and make operations more efficient and secure, but it also raises concerns related to security, privacy and ethics.
